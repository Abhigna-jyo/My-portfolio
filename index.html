About Me

I photograph
truth, not perfection.

Hi, I'm Abhigna — a Hyderabad-based freelance photographer with a deep love for documentary, portrait, and fine-art photography. I believe the best images are found in unguarded moments, when the light is imperfect and the laughter is real.

My work has appeared in national publications, weddings across South India, and editorial campaigns for lifestyle brands. Every frame I create is a deliberate act of storytelling — quiet, honest, and deeply personal.
